Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

At Big-O Notation Estimator, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our website and services. Our goal is to provide a transparent and secure experience for all users.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data from users of the Big-O Notation Estimator. The tool operates entirely client-side, meaning all code analysis happens in your browser. No data you input into the text area is sent to our servers or stored by us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information or store your input, we do not use your data for any purpose. The code you enter is processed locally on your device to estimate time complexity and is not transmitted elsewhere.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies to monitor your activity on our site. Any third-party scripts (e.g., Cloudflare for security) are used solely to protect the website and do not track individual user behavior.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may include third-party scripts (e.g., Cloudflare) to enhance security and performance. These services may collect anonymized data, such as IP addresses, for their own purposes (e.g., bot protection). Please refer to Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy for more details on their practices.

5. Data Security

Since we do not store your data, there is no risk of data breaches from our end. The analysis tool is designed to be lightweight and secure, running entirely within your browser.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us via our Feedback page. We’re here to assist you!
