About Big-O Notation Estimator

The Big-O Notation Estimator is a free, user-friendly tool designed to help developers understand and analyze the time complexity of their JavaScript functions. Our mission is to empower developers to write efficient, scalable code by providing instant feedback on algorithmic performance.

We believe that understanding Big-O notation is key to mastering software development. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an experienced coder optimizing complex systems, our tool simplifies the process of estimating time complexity, making it accessible to all.

Launched in 2024, this project reflects our commitment to advancing developer education and fostering a community of continuous learners. Have feedback? We’d love to hear from you!
